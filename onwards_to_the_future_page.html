Onwards to the Future

Project Type

Creative Fantasy Drawing

Date

2020

Material

Prismacolor Pencil, Ink Pen, Bristol Paper

Inspiration

Learning about the advances in technology in the past century

Description

The train emerges from the past, carrying echoes of ancient buildings and bustling figures, and ushers us into the future where ethereal ghostly figures of artificial intelligence roam freely. As the train glides forward, it bears testimony to human progress, revealing the relentless pursuit of innovation.
