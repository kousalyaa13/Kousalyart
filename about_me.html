  • About Me

Hello! My name is Kousalya Potti. I believe art is a powerful way to connect with others and share feelings, even beyond language and culture. Art lets us share experiences, ideas, and viewpoints in a special way that words can't always do. This helps us understand and empathize with people from different backgrounds.

For me, art is more than just a hobby; it's how I express myself deeply. I've grown a lot as an artist, from winning early art competitions to creating award-winning pieces about mental health, growing up, social standards, the COVID-19 pandemic, and more. My portfolio has a variety of multimedia works that show the complexity of our world and the beauty of life. Take a look at my portfolio and let my art take you on a journey that is thoughtful, inspiring, and uplifting.
