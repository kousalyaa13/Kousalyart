Project Type

Creative Portrait

Date

2022

Material

Charcoal, Prismacolor Pencil, Ink Pen, Gel Pen

Award

Scholastic Art & Writing Awards: Honorable Mention (2023-24)

Description

Rainbow tears symbolize the collective pain and resilience of the LGBTQ+ community, enduring sorrow and demonstrating strength and unity amidst adversity, showcasing the diversity and emotions felt by this community. The figurine symbolizes collective effort and solidarity, representing the tireless work of activists, allies, and the community in promoting acceptance and respect over discrimination. The hard realities must be taken into account, and we must fight for an inclusive society where injustice is recognized and actively worked to end.
