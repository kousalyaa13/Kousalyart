Clouded Depression

Project Type

Creative Real-Model Figure Drawing

Date

2023

Material

Charcoal, Chalk Pastel

Inspiration

My close friend Mira who battled depression.

Description

Mira's world is painted in shades of gray, the color of depression gripping the human spirit. As a dear friend, I offered support and understanding, and hope that with recovery, this moment will be only a memory. This experience taught me that raising mental health awareness and fostering compassion can brighten even the grayest days.
