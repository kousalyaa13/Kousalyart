Project Type

Remodeled Creative Portrait

Date

Original - 2019; Recrafted - 2023

Material

Oil Pastel, Prismacolor Pencil, Markers

Inspiration

Originally, these were two different pieces. But later, I wanted to dedicate a piece to showing the layers of femininity in society, thus I arranged the satin drapery in a way to show the free-flowing behavior of women.

Description

For me, pears symbolize the many faces of womanhood, the feminine contours, fertility and caregiving. As they ripen, they change, much like women growing wiser with experience. Pears also remind me of the importance of women's multifaceted roles and contributions to our world.
