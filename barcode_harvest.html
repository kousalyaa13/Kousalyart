Inspiration

I found myself in the fruit section, eyeing a pile of vibrant oranges. It was a simple task, really â€“ just picking up a few of these juicy fruits. But as I reached for them, my gaze shifted to the barcode stickers adorning each piece.

Description

In our fast-paced, consumer-driven society, products are reduced to lifeless numbers and symbols. It is crucial to recognize the innate worth of these goods, to grasp that beyond the pursuit of profit and efficiency, lies the profound beauty and significance of the natural world.
